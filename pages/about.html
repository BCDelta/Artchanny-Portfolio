About Me

Artchanny

Hello my name is Artchanny, a digital-artist specializing creating voxel art. I have been working with MagicaVoxel since 2020 and have been loving the process of making art with the program. My favorite aspect is the real-time rendering MagicaVoxel provides and how pretty the lighting effects are. For this reason, I mainly focus my artwork on sci-fi scenes. While I like working in MagicaVoxel's boundary of 2563 voxels, I also can create quite large scenes in terms of voxel count using a combination of both WorldPainter and FileToVox.

When I first started working in MagicaVoxel, I never would've thought that my art would lead me to doing commission-based work. As of now, I have done over 20 small projects for individuals and have even worked with several large-scale projects such as:
