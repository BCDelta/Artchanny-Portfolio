INSTAGRAM

I started out my voxel-art journey with Instagram back in 2019. I had received a drawing tablet for my birthday from my friends and decided to pursue digital-art. Initially, the posts were just of me posting my progress and some small projects I did for fun. However, the account really start to grow in 2020 when I posted a small voxel bathroom I made in Magicavoxel. I shifted my art-medium over to voxel-art and have been enjoying it ever since.
